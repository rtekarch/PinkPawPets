about us

Two pet people who happen to also work yachts.

When we're stateside, this is what we do — and we love it. 5+ years and hundreds of pets and homes. Overnight boarding, in-home pet sitting, house watch, daily visits, the works. We chose this because animals are easier to love than spreadsheets.

The other half of our year, we work as crew leads on private yachts — the kind of boats with names like "Persephone" and helipads. That world is unforgiving about the small stuff: every code remembered, every key tracked, every detail kept. We bring those habits home with us. Your dog gets the snuggles. Your home gets the attention.

Your pet doesn't know you're 4,000 miles away. We make sure your home doesn't either.
