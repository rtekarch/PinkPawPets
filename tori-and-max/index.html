About Tori & Max

Two pet people who happen to also work yachts.

When we're stateside, this is what we do. 5+ years and hundreds of pets and homes — overnight boarding, in-home pet sitting, house watch, daily visits, the works. We chose this because we love animals first, paychecks second.

The other half of our year, we work as crew leads on private yachts — the ones you've seen in port photos. That world is unforgiving about the small stuff: schedules kept, spaces tidy, guests at ease. We bring those habits home with us. Your dog gets the snuggles. Your home gets the attention to detail.

Your pet doesn't know you're 4,000 miles away. We make sure your home doesn't either.
