About Tori & Max

The standards of a private yacht brought home.

For half the year, Tori & Max work as crew leads aboard private yachts — the ones you see anchored off St. Barts and the Amalfi Coast. The expectations there are uncompromising. Schedules are kept. Spaces are pristine. Guests are at ease.

When they're stateside, they bring the same discipline to your driveway. Whether it's a three-night sit with a senior dog or a two-week house watch with mail, plants, and a finicky cat, every visit is logged, photographed, and delivered with the kind of quiet professionalism you usually have to charter a 140-foot yacht to get.

“Your pet doesn't know you're 4,000 miles away. We make sure your home doesn't either.”

Services

Premium care, tailored to your trip.

Most clients combine two or three of these. Pricing is custom and quoted after a free meet-and-greet — never a surprise.

Overnight Boarding

Your pet stays with us in a quiet, fully pet-proofed home. Crate-trained or free-roam. One booking per family at a time — never a kennel.

From dusk to dawn · 24/7 attention

In-Home Pet Sitting

We stay overnight at your place. Your pet's routine, bed, food, and view all stay the same. We handle feedings, meds, walks, and playtime.

Per night · Multi-pet welcome

House Sitting

For homes without pets. Mail, packages, plants, lights, alarm checks, trash pickup — and a real human inside the house each day. Insurance-friendly.

Daily checks · Custom routine

Daily Care Visits

Drop-in visits — feeding, fresh water, litter, a real walk (not just a yard break), and yes, photos every time. 30 or 60-minute options.

1–4 visits per day

Senior & Special Needs

Travel-Day Concierge
